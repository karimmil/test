About us

• No chemical

Healthy Fresh Microgreens is an urban farm located in Meadowvale Village - Mississauga.

We specialize in producing fresh, organically grown and hand raised microgreens.

Grown and harvested to order, we guarantee freshness.

All of our microgreens are specially produced for each order and delivered weekly to home, work and restaurants.

We pride ourselves on being a planet-friendly business. We are completely chemical-free. Our seeds are 100% certified organic.
